# Outputs

CityBEM generates hourly and aggregated outputs for building energy, solar radiation, and PV systems.

## 1. Building Energy Outputs
- Indoor air temperature  
- Heating load  
- Cooling load  
- Surface heat fluxes  

## 2. Radiation Outputs
- Shaded/unshaded irradiance  
- Surface shading factors  
- Beam and diffuse components  

## 3. PV Outputs
- Hourly PV power  
- Annual PV yield  
- Energy self-sufficiency index  

## 4. Microclimate Outputs
- Corrected heat transfer coefficients  
- Surface temperature changes  

## 5. Export Formats
- TXT 
- CSV  
- Hourly/Minute time series
